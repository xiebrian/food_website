H31 Meal Plan

View from the MacGregor H31 suite kitchen

Welcome to the H31 Meal Plan (H31MP) webpage!

H31 Meal Plan is an informal pop-up restaurant organized by myself located by MacGregor House. The name H31 comes from my suite -- I live in H Entry, suite 31. This is a personal project that I've wanted to try for a long time. After some thought and consideration, I've decided to do a trial run over IAP.

My motivation for opening H31MP is not for profit. I see this as an incredible learning opportunity: learning new dishes from the requests that I get, experimenting with cooking techniques, and gaining experience cooking for larger groups of people. I also want to make this an opportunity to meet new people and connect with friends in the MIT community. With that in mind, H31MP is completely free. Don't worry about expenses. Don't worry if you feel that you don't know me very well (or at all) - I want to get to know more people here! All that you would need to do is show up.

If you are interested, please fill out the Signup Form -- it'll contain more logistical information.

Currently, I do not have any additional availability over IAP, but stay updated for if I decide to continue this into the spring semester. Please feel free to reach out to me via email (brianxie@mit.edu) or through Facebook Messenger if you're interested or have any questions!

Cheers, and I look forward to meeting you all!
